ECO-Series: Pakistan's First Water-Safe Textile Auxiliary Chemical Line

📅 May 2025 ✍️ Seamens Enterprises ⏱ 6 min read

Since 1997, Seamens Enterprises has been committed to one mission: delivering world-class chemical solutions to Pakistan's textile industry. The ECO-Series is the culmination of 25+ years of research, field experience, and a deep understanding of what Pakistani mills truly need — performance, consistency, and environmental responsibility.

What Makes ECO-Series Different?

Most textile auxiliaries available in Pakistan are either imported at high cost or manufactured locally with inconsistent quality. The ECO-Series fills this gap — locally manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 45001 quality and safety standards, using water-safe formulations that reduce effluent load and comply with international environmental regulations.

Every batch is tested before dispatch. Every product carries a full Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS). And every customer receives dedicated technical support from our field team.

The Complete ECO-Series Product Range

Sizing & Desizing

  • ECOSIZE-CXW-111 — Premium warp sizing agent. Excellent film formation, high adhesion to cotton and polyester blends, easy enzymatic removal. Available in 25 KGS bags.
  • ECODESIZE-EZ — High-efficiency enzymatic desizer for rapid, complete size removal with low water consumption.

Wetting, Scouring & Leveling

  • ECOSHORT-GS-777 — Fast-wetting surfactant for rapid, even liquor penetration. Available in 225 KGS plastic drums.
  • ECOSCOUR-SCA-30 — Powerful scouring agent for effective removal of sizing, oils, and natural impurities from grey fabric.
  • ECOWET+DET — Combined wetting and detergent action for pre-treatment and washing processes.
  • ECOLEVEL-LV — Leveling agent for uniform, streak-free dyeing across all fibre types.
  • ECODYE-LEV Series — Advanced dye leveling series for reactive, disperse, and vat dye systems.

Softeners & Finishing

  • ECOSOFTENER D-11 — Silicone-free softener delivering a natural, smooth hand feel without affecting absorbency. Ideal for towels and home textiles.
  • ECOSIL-SS — Premium silicone softener for ultra-soft, luxurious fabric finish. Available in 240 KGS plastic drums.

Functional Finishes

  • ECOAcryllic ACL-55 — High-performance acrylic binder for pigment printing and coating applications. Available in 240 KGS drums and 1000 KGS IBC tanks.
  • ECO-Weight Enhancer WKL-121 — Weight-building auxiliary for value addition in finished fabric. Enhances drape and body.
  • ECOPVA-GS — PVA-based sizing/finishing agent for improved stiffness and surface smoothness. Available in 240 KGS drums.
  • ECOSHIELD-WP — Water-proof finishing agent for outdoor and technical textile applications. Available in 50 & 220 KGS drums.
  • ECOGAURD-FP — Fire-proof / flame retardant finishing chemical. Available in 50 KGS and 220 KGS plastic drums.
  • ECOFLAME-FR PRO — Imported premium flame retardant for high-specification FR fabric requirements.

Fixers & Colour Fastness

  • ECOFIX-FB — Formaldehyde-free reactive dye fixer for improved wash fastness ratings. Available in 50 KGS and 220 KGS drums.
  • ECOSTAB-HS — High-stability fixative for improved colour fastness in dyed and printed fabrics.

Packaging & Nationwide Availability

All ECO-Series products are available in 25 KGS bags, 225–240 KGS plastic drums, and 1000 KGS IBC tanks for large-scale mill operations. Products are stocked at our Lahore factory and dispatched nationwide within 24–48 hours.

Technical Support Included

Every ECO-Series customer receives complimentary on-site technical consultation, application trials, dosage optimisation, and regular field visits from our qualified chemical team. We do not just sell chemicals — we ensure they work correctly in your specific process conditions.
